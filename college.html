What and why Engineering?

Engineering is the application of scientific principles to design, build, and maintain structures, machines, and processes. It spans various fields, each contributing uniquely to technological and societal advancements.Engineering drives progress in every industry, from transportation and healthcare to communication and space exploration. Engineers solve real-world problems, making life easier, safer, and more efficient

About Amal Jyothi College Of Engineering

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally, is the first engineering college in Kerala to obtain NAAC accreditation with ‘A’ grade, and the first new generation engineering college in the State to secure the prestigious NBA accreditation for prime departments. The main features of the College comprise world-class infrastructure, top-flight faculty, high pass percentage, excellent placement record, unique student projects and first rate start-ups. Amal Jyothi is a mega complex of 16.5 lakh sq.ft. built-up area overlooking the busy Kanjirapally-Erumeli stretch of the Kottayam-Sabarimala state highway. The two campus hostels, taken care of by dedicated Catholic priests and sisters, have a capacity of 2500. The campus has 2.04 Gbps internet connectivity with WiFi.

Core Branches of Engineering

  • Mechanical Engineering: Focuses on designing and manufacturing machines and mechanical systems.
  • Electrical Engineering: Deals with electrical systems, circuits, and power generation.
  • Computer Engineering: Combines computer science and electronic engineering to develop hardware and software.
  • Civil Engineering: Involves the construction of buildings, roads, and infrastructure projects.
  • Chemical Engineering:Deals with chemical reactions,materials science and process design.

Library of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering

The college also has a well-equipped Central Library, which serves as a hub of knowledge for students and faculty. The library houses an extensive collection of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources covering various fields of engineering, science, and technology. It provides access to e-journals, online databases, and study materials to support academic and research activities. The library’s digital section ensures students have access to the latest resources, promoting a culture of continuous learning.
